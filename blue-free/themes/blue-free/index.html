About UEI

The UEI™ Group is a world leader in the manufacture of engraved metal products and associated engraving supplies as well as a distributor of stamping foil, decorative glitter and makeready supplies for use in graphic finishing and related industries. Our products are utilized to decorate, enhance, and add value to a wide range of diverse products.

Our member companies continually strive to provide the highest quality products from brass & copper embossing dies to state-of-the-art photoetching engraving machines to metallic hot stamping foil.

Building on 70-years of collective industry expertise, and a long and rich heritage of product innovation in the marketplace, the UEI Group companies have forged ahead as the leaders, providing countless solutions and opportunities to the industries which we serve.

UIE and Covid 19

Universal Engraving, Inc. is an “Essential Business” that provides key raw material components to customers who serve the pharmaceutical, medical, hygiene, food and beverage industries. We will remain open to manufacture/supply our needed products. This is an ever-changing situation, and we will continue to monitor our responsibilities to our valued employees, customers and communities. Should any changes be necessary in our operational status, we will act accordingly and update our company statement.
